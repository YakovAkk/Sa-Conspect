# Coupling and Cohesion

Monolithic and distributed architectural styles are strongly influenced by coupling and cohesion, and weak cohesion often leads to undesirable strong coupling (and vice versa).

Strong architecture favors high cohesion with loose coupling, making systems easier to maintain, deploy, and evolve through continuous updates.
